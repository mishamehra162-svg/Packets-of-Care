About the Project

FOOD SUPPORT THAT RESPECTS DIGNITY

This project was created to make basic food support more accessible. By distributing uncooked packets of rice and lentils, the project helps provide simple, nutritious ingredients that people can cook and use in their own way.

The focus is on dignity, practicality, and community care. The project supports individuals who may be facing financial difficulty, including homeless individuals and students managing tight budgets.

Each packet represents more than food. It represents care, respect, and the belief that small actions can create meaningful support.
